About Us
Pursuing Good Health

Afya for Jamii is a registered community based organization in Malindi that is committed pursue good health in all its forms, and our mission is to reduce maternal mortality rates, lower malnutrition rates, and encourage drug adherence among people living with HIV/AIDS. We operate on the basis of core principles that guide our actions and decisions, including our reliance on God, our commitment to integrity, our focus on critical areas of need, and our respect for all people. By working together, we believe that we can make a meaningful difference in the lives of those we serve.

What we do
Maternal Health Services

The organization should offer maternal health services to reduce maternal mortality rates. These services could include antenatal care, postnatal care, family planning, and delivery services.

Nutrition Programs

To lower malnutrition rates, the organization should offer nutrition programs that promote healthy eating habits, provide information on the importance of proper nutrition, and distribute food to those in need.

HIV/AIDS Support Services

Afya for Jamii should provide services that encourage drug adherence among people living with HIV/AIDS. These services may include education about HIV/AIDS, counseling, testing, and treatment.

Community Outreach and Education

The organization should offer community outreach and education programs to raise awareness about health issues and promote healthy behaviors. This could include health fairs, workshops, and seminars on various health topics.

Advocacy and Policy Development

To address critical areas of need, Afya for Jamii should advocate for policies that promote good health in the community. This may include working with local government and other organizations to improve access to healthcare and to ensure that health services are delivered in a way that is culturally appropriate and respectful.

Faith-Based Support

Afya for Jamii should provide faith-based support to those in need. This could include offering spiritual guidance, prayer, and other forms of emotional and spiritual support to individuals and families.
